Being a volunteer is a rewarding experience. Unfortunately, it can also cost you some money. Thanksgiving is a simple app that allows volunteers to submit their activities and non-profit admins to track them.

France is helping non-profit

You can turn your volunteering activities into training credit and tax deductions.

Features

  • Submit volunteering time in two clicks
  • Track mileage automatically through geo-fencing
  • Approve volunteering activities
  • Generate CERFA tax-deductions forms
  • Supports multiple non-profit organizations, and decentralized
